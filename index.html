About ULFG1

The Faculty of Engineering of the Lebanese University in Tripoli was first established in October 1974 but had its doors closed until 1980 as a result of the ongoing civil war in Lebanon at the time. The main mission of the Faculty of Engineering is to train high-level engineers with expertise in the fields of Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering and Petrochemical Engineering. Courses are accompanied with methodological laboratory practices in order to help engineering students put their knowledge in use.
